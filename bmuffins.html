Blueberry Muffins

Ingredients

  • 225g butter 1,305cal
  • 225g Plain Flour 817cal
  • 125g Caster Sugar 375cal
  • 3tsp Baking Powder 7cal
  • 250g Blueberries 194cal
  • 1 Egg 288cal
  • 100g White Cooking Chocolate 582cal
  • 100ml Double Cream 350cal

Preperation

  1. Preheat oven to 200°.
  2. Line a muffin tin with muffin cases.
  3. Whisk dry ingredients together in a large bowl.
  4. Add butter and eggs and whisk until just combined.
  5. Fold in 3/4 of blueberries (cut in half).
  6. Spoon batter into muffin cases.
  7. Bake for 15-20 minutes.
  8. Remove from tin and cool on wire rack.
  9. Heat double cream until it bubbles and add chopped chocolate (67ml of milk and 17g of butter can substitute for 100ml double cream).
  10. Once ganache is cooled spoon over muffins and place remaining blueberries on top.
