Oat Bread

Ingredients

  • 500g Natural Yoghurt 345cal
  • 400g Oats 1,480cal
  • 1 Large Egg cal
  • 1 Banana 80cal
  • 2 tsp Bicarbonate of Soda
  • 4 tsp Pecan Nut 217cal
  • 3 tsp Walnuts 140cal
  • 3 tsp Brazil Nuts 207cal
  • 4 tsp Sunflower Seeds 103cal
  • 3 tsp Chia Seeds 72cal
  • 3 tsp Pumpkin Seeds 118cal

Preperation

  1. Preheat oven at 160°.
  2. Pour Yoghurt into large bowl and add oats, bicarbonate of soda, nuts, seeds and banana (mashed).
  3. Butter a tin and add ingredients.
  4. Bake for 1 hour approximately
  5. Remove from tin and cool on wire rack.
