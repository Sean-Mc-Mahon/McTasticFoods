Porridge

Ingredients

  • 40g Oats 148cal
  • 140ml Whole Milk 88cal
  • 15g Honey 64cal
  • 1 Banana 80cal
  • 4 Strawberries 22cal
  • 40g Blueberries 28cal

Preperation

  1. Add milk to oats and microwave on high for 2 minutes stirring after 1 minute.
  2. Chop banana and strawberries and add to porridge along with blueberries.
  3. Pour honey over porridge.
