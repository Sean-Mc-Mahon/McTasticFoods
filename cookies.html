Cookies

Ingredients

  • 415g Plain Flower 400cal
  • 1.5 tsp Baking Powder 5cal
  • 1.25 tsp Bicarbonate of Soda 6cal
  • 1.5 tsp Salt Flakes
  • 280g Unsalted Butter 700cal
  • 250g Soft Light Brown Sugar 1,120cal
  • 200g Granulated Sugar 1,000cal
  • 2 Large Eggs 100cal
  • 2 tsp Vanilla Extract 30cal
  • 150g Milk Chocolate 802cal
  • 150g White Chocolate 750cal
  • 100g Chopped Pecans 700cal

Preperation

  1. Preheat oven at 180°.
  2. Whisk flour, baking powder, bicarbonate of soda and salt flakes.
  3. Mix butter and sugars, beat in eggs one at a time and add vanilla extract. Add in dry ingredients and mix until just combined.
  4. Add chocolate chips and pecans, wrap mix in cling film and place in fridge overnight.
  5. Scoop golfball sized balls onto tray and heat in oven for 9mins.
  6. Remove from oven and cool on wire rack.
