Flapjacks

Ingredients

  • 115g Butter 824cal
  • 100g Golden Castor Sugar 389cal
  • 75g Golden Syrup 287cal
  • 1 tsp Vanilla Extract 15cal
  • 250g oats 888cal

Preperation

  1. Preheat oven at 180° and line a 20x30cm tin with baking paper.
  2. Combine butter, sugar, syrup and vanilla extract in saucepan and melt over medium heat.
  3. Pour mixture over oats in large bowland stir until all oats are coated.
  4. Pour mixture into lined tin and spread evenly, pressing firmly.
  5. Bake for 20mins or until golden brown, score into 16 pieces.
