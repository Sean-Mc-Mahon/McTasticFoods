Vegan Thai Green Curry

Ingredients

  • 150g Mange Tout 58cal
  • 120g Cashews 686cal
  • Handful Basil Leaves 3cal
  • 1 Tbsp of Olive Oil 119cal
  • 400g Can of Cocunut Milk 652cal
  • 210g Rice 733cal

Paste

  • Thumb of Ginger 6cal
  • 4 Cloves of Garlic 18cal
  • 3 Green Chillies 6cal
  • Stalks of Basil 3cal
  • 1 Tbsp of Coriander 16cal
  • 1 Tbsp of Cumin 30cal
  • 1 Tbsp of Honey 64cal
  • 4 Tbsp of Soy Sauce 80cal
  • Juice of 2 Limes 15cal
  • Water from Can of Coconut Milk

Preperation

  1. Boil 210g of Rice.
  2. Chop garlic, ginger, green chillies, basil stalks and add to blender.
  3. Add coriander, cumin, honey, lemon juice, soy sauce, water from coconut milk and blend.
  4. Add oil to wok and add paste and coconut milk.
  5. Add cashews and mangetout and serve over rice.
  6. Garnish with basil leaves.
