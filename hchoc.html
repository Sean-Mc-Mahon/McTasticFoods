Hot Chocolate

Ingredients

  • 170ml Whole Milk 109cal
  • 1tsp Chocolate Powder 28cal
  • 1tsp Granulated Sugar 16cal
  • 10g Whipped Cream 29cal

Preperation

  1. Add milk to sugar and chocolate powder and microwave for 100 seconds stirring halfway through.
  2. Add whipped cream.
