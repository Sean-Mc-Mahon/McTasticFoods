Oven Chips

Ingredients

  • 400g Potatoe 300cal
  • 1 Tbsp Olive Oil 119cal
  • 1 tbsp Thyme
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 1 tsp Pepper

Preperation

  1. Leave skin on potatoe, cut into shape of fries 10mm by 10mm.
  2. Transfer to large bowl and soak in water for 30 minutes,
    drain, rinse and dry with a tea towel.
  3. Transfer back to bowl and add tbsp of olive oil,
    add salt, pepper, thyme, basil and parsley and mix.
  4. Spread over baking tray and cook at 240 degrees for 30 minutes.
