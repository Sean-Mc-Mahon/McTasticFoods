Chilli

Ingredients

  • 2 Sweet Potatoes 260cal
  • 1 Tbsp Olive Oil 119cal
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 1 tsp Pepper
  • 1 tsp Cayenne Pepper
  • 0.5 tsp Cumin
  • 0.5 tsp Coriander
  • Coriander Stalks
  • 2 Chillies
  • 2 Chillies
  • 4 Cloves of Garlic
  • 2 Peppers
  • 2 tins of Kidney Beans
  • 2 tins of Tomatoes

Preperation

  1. Cut two Sweet potatoes to chunks and seasoned with salt, pepper, cayenne pepper and cumin, put in oven for 30 minutes at 240 degrees.
  2. Heat olive oil in pot and add 4 chopped cloves of garlic and two chopped peppers and season with cayenne pepper (1tsp), cinnamon (0.5 tsp) and cumin (0.5 tsp), finely sliced coriander stalks and finely slice 2 chillies add two tins of kidney beans and two tins of tomatoes and some water.
  3. Simmer for 30 minutes, it should reduce by a third.
  4. Add sweet potatoes, stir in some chopped coriander and serve with rice.
